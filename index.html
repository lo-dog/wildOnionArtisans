About us

Welcome to Wild Onion Artisans, a pottery studio and artist co-op in Logan Lake, B.C. featuring unique individual artworks created by myself and my talented family. Between us we have a wide range of art represented showcasing our individual styles.

About me

Sandra Davis

I am multi media artist focusing on pottery, glasswork, basketry, and wild crafting. I started my career with a strong fine arts background in graphics, photography and Industrial design. From there, with a love of wild crafting and herbal remedies we created a herb farm in Brackendale, BC making tinctures, herbal blends and jellies. Moving to Revelstoke gave pause and introduced stained glass and pottery. Logan Lake saw me fulfill my dream of becoming a full time potter. I now have taken this medium and interweave pottery with glass, nature and wildcrafting. Custom orders are my specialty; realizing the clients dream into a functional piece of art.
